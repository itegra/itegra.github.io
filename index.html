EZETERA We are preparing your
return to school

Product Description

Ezetera.com is part of the GEA group and the largest on-line school supply company in Mexico. Ezetera.com provides school textbooks, uniforms and all types of stationery, backpacks and kids clothing to schools throughout Mexico. Ezetera generates the vast majority of it’s business during their very busy back-to-school season which runs from June-August. This requires high-performance and high-reliability along with dedicated support.

Project Description

Following a code review of Ezetera’s Magento Enterprise site as well as their overall architecture and infrastructure, ITEGRATION proposed a significant set of recommendations ahead of Ezetera’s busy back-to-school season in 2015. Ezetera knew that significant improvements were needed ahead of their most important sales period.

ITEGRATION upgraded Ezetera to Magento 1.14 and introduced many functional and performance changes to both the back and front end on inherited code. In addition, a review of Ezetera infrastructure resulted in a 75% cost savings on an annual basis.

After launching the new Ezetera.com ahead of schedule and ready for the back-to-school season, ITEGRATION supported Ezetera throughout their critical busy season and the site performed tremendously, well ahead of expectations and previous years.

The result was a real step-change in the quality of Ezetera.com and the efficiency of the on-line operation.
